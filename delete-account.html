SmartLog - Account & Data Deletion

Thank you for using SmartLog - Pro Receipt Scan. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated receipt and financial data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Please send us an account deletion request using the email address you registered with on SmartLog.

  • Email Subject: Account Deletion Request for SmartLog
  • Email Body: Please state your registered email address and confirm that you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete your account information and all scanned data from our servers within 3-5 business days.

Please note: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your receipts and financial logs cannot be recovered.
